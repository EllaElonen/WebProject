Video

The <audio> and <video> tags can be used for adding audio and video files to your HTML page. You need to have two versions of the same file. The files below were downloaded from Freesound The free convertor at Zamzar was used in order to make two different files. Te code for adding the video looks like this, and is similar for the audiofile, just replace video with audio.

<video width="400" controls>
<source src="filename.mp4" type="video/mp4">
<source src="filename.ogg" type="video/ogg">
</video>
